# groupcup

A Telegram-native World Cup prediction league for your group chat.

- **Track:** Consumer & Fan Experiences
- **Prize:** $16K (10k / 4k / 2k)
- **Chain:** Solana (identity) · **Data:** TxLINE (primary input)

## What it is

Add the bot to your friends' Telegram group. It drops match-prediction polls, locks them at kickoff, and **auto-scores the leaderboard live from the TxLINE SSE stream** — right in the chat, no spreadsheet, no referee. Solana wallet for identity; optional on-chain pot.

## Why it wins

A mainstream fan would actually use it: it lives where fans already are (the group chat), updates in real time, and has a clear monetization path (pot rake, premium leagues).
